Followsweep 🚀

📘 How to Use

  1. Go to Instagram → Settings → Accounts Center
  2. Download Your Information
  3. Select only Followers & Following
  4. Choose format as JSON
  5. Upload files below
  6. Click "Show Unfollowers"
  7. Click Unfollow → — opens Instagram in one tab (reused every time)
  8. Unfollow them, come back here, click ✔ Done — auto opens next
